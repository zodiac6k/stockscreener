Bridge Pose (Setu Bandhasana)

Description

Strengthens core and glutes while opening hips

Benefits

  • Tones abs
  • Strengthens back
  • Opens hips
  • Improves digestion

Instructions

  1. Lie on back with knees bent
  2. Lift hips toward ceiling
  3. Keep shoulders on ground
  4. Hold for 30-60 seconds

Difficulty: Beginner   |   Duration: 30-60 seconds
