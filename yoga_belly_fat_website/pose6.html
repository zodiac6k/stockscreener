Side Plank (Vasisthasana)

Description

Targets obliques and strengthens entire core

Benefits

  • Tones obliques
  • Strengthens arms
  • Improves balance
  • Burns fat

Instructions

  1. Start in plank position
  2. Rotate to one side
  3. Stack feet and lift arm
  4. Hold for 15-30 seconds each side

Difficulty: Intermediate   |   Duration: 15-30 seconds each side
