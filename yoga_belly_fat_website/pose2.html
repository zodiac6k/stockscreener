Boat Pose (Navasana)

Description

Excellent for strengthening abdominal muscles

Benefits

  • Tones abs
  • Improves digestion
  • Strengthens back
  • Enhances balance

Instructions

  1. Sit with knees bent
  2. Lift feet off ground
  3. Extend arms parallel to ground
  4. Hold for 15-30 seconds

Difficulty: Intermediate   |   Duration: 15-30 seconds
