Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

Description

Strengthens back and tones abdominal muscles

Benefits

  • Tones belly
  • Improves posture
  • Opens chest
  • Relieves back pain

Instructions

  1. Lie on stomach
  2. Place hands under shoulders
  3. Lift chest off ground
  4. Hold for 15-30 seconds

Difficulty: Beginner   |   Duration: 15-30 seconds
