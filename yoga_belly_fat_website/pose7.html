Crow Pose (Bakasana)

Description

Advanced pose that requires strong core engagement

Benefits

  • Strengthens core
  • Builds arm strength
  • Improves balance
  • Burns calories

Instructions

  1. Squat with feet together
  2. Place hands on ground
  3. Lift feet off ground
  4. Balance on hands

Difficulty: Advanced   |   Duration: 10-20 seconds
